Bihar STET Answer Key 2025 Notice

The candidates who appeared in the Secondary Teacher Eligibility Test (STET) 2025 held from 14.10.2025 to 16.11.2025 are hereby informed that the Response Sheet along with the Answer Key of the said examination will be uploaded on the website of the Committee https://bsebstet.org from the afternoon of 24.11.2025 to 27.11.2025. The concerned candidates can view their Response Sheet through their User ID and Password.

If any candidate feels that the answer to any question in the answer key of the said Response Sheet is incorrect, then the concerned candidate can register his objection through online medium within the stipulated period by clicking on the link "Click here for Objection STET 2025" given on the said website of the board. To raise objection on the answer key, it is mandatory for the concerned candidates to pay the prescribed fee of Rs. 50/- per question through Debit Card/ Credit Card/ Net Banking.

How To Download STET Answer Key 2025

There are following simple steps for online downloading of Bihar Secondary Teacher Eligibility Test 2025 answer key as under described:

  1. Visit Bihar Secondary Teacher Eligibility Test STET 2025 official website i.e. https://bsebstet.org
  2. In the middle of Home Page under Important Link section click on the given link as "Click here to Download Answer Key for SECONDARY TEACHER ELIGIBILITY TEST (STET - 2025)".
  3. You will be redirected to answer key download page of Bihar Secondary Teacher Eligibility Test 2025.
  4. In the Answer Key Login page enter your Application No./ Login ID and PASSWORD correctly in the specified input field.
  5. Next enter Security Pin as shown in the page correctly to proceed further.
  6. Next click on Login button to access your Dashboard.
  7. Finally click on Login button to download answer key from BSEB STET server.
