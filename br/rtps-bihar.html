RTPS Bihar Apply Online Method

  1. serviceonline.bihar.gov.in वेबसाइट पर जाना |
  2. अगर आवेदक नये User हैं, तो "Register Yourself से Login ID एवं Password बनाना |
  3. वेबसाइट के Left Menu से संबंधित विभाग की बांधित Service पर Click कर लॉगिन (Login) करना |
  4. Left Menu से "Apply for Service पर Click करना | आबेदन फॉर्म को ध्यान से भरना, Webcam या File Browser से अपना फोटो अपलोड करना, आवश्यक सुधार करना एवं [Save Draft] करना |
  5. (a) सेवा विशेष के लिए आधार नंबर मांगे जाने पर आवेदक के आधार में पंजीकृत मोबाइल नंबर पर OTP भेज कर सत्यापन कराया जाना, या (b) भारत निर्वाचन आयोग द्वारा स्वीकृत 12 पहचान पत्रों में से कोई एक upload करना |
  6. सेवा विशेष के लिए मांगे जाने पर जरुरी अनुलग्नकों (Annexures) को [Attach] करना |
  7. आवेदन फॉर्म एवं Annexure List को ध्यानपूर्वक पढ़ना एवं आवश्यक सुधार करना |
  8. आवेदन को [Submit] कर अपना पावती (Acknowledgement) डाउनलोड / प्रिंट करना |
  9. प्रमाण-पत्र (Certificate) बन जाने के पश्चात आवेदक के Inbox में उपलब्ध हो जाना, जिसे आवेदक द्वारा बेबसाइट पर Login करके डाउनलोड / प्रिंट करना |

How To Download RTPS Bihar Certificate

There are following simple steps for downloading of certificates online at RTPS Bihar portal as follows:

  1. At first open RTPS Bihar official website serviceonline.bihar.gov.in
  2. In the right side of Home Page under Citizen Section click on the given link as "Download Certificate"
  3. Next you will be redirected to the Download Certificate page of RTPS.
  4. Select Services from drop down menu as RTPS/ Others.
  5. Enter Application Ref. Number (eg: BICCO/2021/0000) and Applicant Name (In English).
  6. Finally click on Download Certificate button to download your certificate from RTPS server 1, 2, 3, 4, 5 and so on.
  7. Note: Please download your certificate only after 24 hours of execution of your application.

RTPS Bihar Application Status

There are following simple steps for online checking of application status at RTPS Bihar portal as follows:

  1. At first open RTPS Bihar official website serviceonline.bihar.gov.in
  2. In the right side of Home Page under Citizen Section click on the given link as "Track Application Status"
  3. Next you will be redirected to the Track Application Status page of RTPS.
  4. Select an option to track application : Through Application Reference Number or Through OTP/ Application Details.
  5. Enter Application Reference Number correctly in the specified input field and choose Track through Application Submission Date or Application Delivery Date.
  6. Next enter Word Verification Code as shown in the page correctly to proceed further.
  7. Finally click on Submit button to check your application status from RTPS server 1, 2, 3 and so on.

FAQs : RTPS Bihar

  • Q.1. What is full form of RTPS?
  • Ans:- The full form of RTPS is Right to Public Service.
  • Q.2. What is RTPS Bihar?
  • Ans:- Bihar Right To Public Service (RTPS) Act and other Services are configured on ServicePlus software framework of NIC for online operation. It is providing the service of Cast Certificate, Income Certificate and Residential Certificate to people of Bihar. The applicants informed by SMS under his certificate is ready for collection from RTPS counter or Online.
  • Q.3 How to download RTPS Bihar certificate?
  • Ans:- Applicants can download online certificate at RTPS Bihar official website by clicking on the given link as Download Certificate through Application Ref. Number and Applicant Name.
  • Q.4. How to check RTPS Bihar application status?
  • Ans:- Applicants can check online status at RTPS Bihar official website by clicking on the given link as Track Application Status through Application Reference Number and Application Submission/ Delivery Date.
  • Q.5. What is RTPS Bihar official website link?
  • Ans:- https://serviceonline.bihar.gov.in
  • Q.6. What is RTPS helpline email?
  • Ans:- Helpline Email : serviceonline.bihar@gov.in
