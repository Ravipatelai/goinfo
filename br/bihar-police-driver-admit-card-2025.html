Bihar Police Driver Exam City 2025 Date

Candidates can get information about the district of the examination centre from 25.11.2025 on the Board's official website https://csbc.bihar.gov.in by entering their registration number or mobile number registered with the application and date of birth.

Bihar Police Driver Admit Card 2025 Notice

E-Admit Card of the candidates for the Driver Constable written examination 2025 will be available on the Board's website https://csbc.bihar.gov.in from 03.12.2025 onwards. Candidates should download their e-admit card and take a print out. To appear in the examination at the examination centre, you will have to produce a valid photo identity card like Aadhar Card, Passport, Driving License, PAN Card or Voter ID Card along with the e-Admit Card.

Bihar Police Constable Driver Exam Syllabus 2025

In the syllabus of the written examination, 60 percent questions will be asked related to general knowledge and current issues and the remaining 40 percent questions will include questions related to traffic signs and signals (20 percent) under the Motor Act and other relevant rules and regulations and general knowledge of parts, lubricating and maintenance of various vehicles etc. and questions related to mechanical and technical faults occurring in vehicles (20 percent). The syllabus can be seen on the Board's website https://csbc.bihar.gov.in.

The written examination will be of 100 marks. A two-hour question paper will contain a total of 100 questions, with one mark awarded for each correct answer. The examination will be based on an OMR sheet. The OMR sheet/answer sheet will be in two copies, one of which will be preserved as an invisible carbon copy by the Central Selection Board for one year after the final selection list is published.

How To Download Bihar Police Driver Admit Card 2025

There are following simple steps for online downloading of Bihar Police Constable driver recruitment examination 2025 admit card as under described:

  1. At first open Central Selection Board of Constable (CSBC) official website https://csbc.bihar.gov.in
  2. In the top of Home Page under Bihar Police section click on the given link as "Click Here To Download e-Admit Card. (Advt. No. 02/2025)".
  3. You will be redirected to Admit Card download page of Recruitment For Bihar Police Driver Constables (Advt. No. 02/2025).
  4. In the Admit Card download page under Download 02/2025 Written Exam Admit Card section enter your Registration ID OR Mobile Number And Date of Birth correctly in the specified input field.
  5. Next enter Captcha Code as shown in the page correctly to proceed further.
  6. Finally click on SUBMIT button to access your dashboard, in this dashboard click on Download Admit Card link to download your admit card for Written Examination 2025.
