Bihar Police Constable Written Exam 2025 Information

After collection of applications, the applications will be scrutinized and a written test will be conducted for those candidates whose applications are found to be correct. The test may be conducted in more than one shift depending on the number of applications received for the advertisement. For multi-session/ multi-paper examinations, normalized marks for determining merit will be calculated using the equi-percentile method.

Bihar Police Constable Exam Scheme 2025

The level of the written exam will be of 10th class or equivalent level of Bihar School Examination Board. The written exam will have objective type questions related to 1. Hindi, 2. English, 3. Mathematics, 4. Social Science (History, Geography, Civics, Economics), 5. Science (Physics, Chemistry, Zoology, Botany), 6. General Knowledge and Current Affairs.

The written exam will be of 100 marks. There will be a total of 100 questions in a two-hour question paper in which one mark will be given for each correct answer. The exam will be based on OMR sheet. The OMR sheet/ answer sheet will be in two copies, out of which one copy will be kept safe with the board as an invisible carbon copy for one year after the final selection list is published.

Bihar Police Constable Merit List 2025

  1. The written exam will not be the basis for the merit list. The written exam will only qualify for the physical efficiency test and document verification.
  2. In case two or more candidates secure equal marks in the physical efficiency test, their position in the merit list will be determined on the basis of their dates of birth, and in case of two or more candidates securing equal marks and having the same date of birth, their educational qualification will be determined. Thus, among the candidates securing equal marks, those having older age and having the same marks and same date of birth will be considered as the candidates.
