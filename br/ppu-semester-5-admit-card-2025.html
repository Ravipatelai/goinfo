PPU Admit Card 2025 Semester 5 Notice

Patliputra University (PPU), Patna has released online admit card of UG semester 5 examination 2025 at its official website https://ppuponline.in before the commencement of examination. All eligible students can download their admit card online from 26th November 2025 onwards through Form Number / Exam Form No. and Date of Birth. UG semester 5 examination shall be started from 01st December 2025 onwards.

How To Download Patliputra University Sem 5 Admit Card 2025

There are following simple steps for online downloading of Patliputra University UG semester 5 examination 2025 Admit Card as under described:

  1. At first visit Patliputra University official website https://ppuponline.in
  2. In the top of Home Page under Examination section click on the given button as "Download Admit Card [UG Semester 5]".
  3. You will be redirected to admit card download page of Patliputra University UG semester 5 examination session 2023-27.
  4. In the admit card download page under Admit Card Search by form number section enter your Form Number / Exam Form No. and Date of Birth correctly in the specified input field.
  5. Next fill Captcha Code as shown in that page correctly to proceed further.
  6. Finally click on Search button to download your admit card and save a copy in PDF or print out for future reference.
