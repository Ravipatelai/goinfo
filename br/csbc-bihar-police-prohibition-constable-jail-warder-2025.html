Bihar Police Prohibition Constable, Jail Warder Eligibility 2025

i. Prohibition Constable / Mobile Squad Constable : 12th pass or Moulvi certificate or Shastri (with English) or Acharya (without English) certificate issued by Sanskrit Board of Bihar State or any other equivalent educational qualification.

ii. Jail Warder : 12th passed or equivalent examination from any Board/ Council recognized by the State Government/ Central Government.

Note:- For the post of Mobile Squad Constable, it is mandatory for the candidate to be a holder of a valid driving license issued by the competent authority to drive two wheeler and four wheeler, light motor vehicle (LMV) or heavy motor vehicle (HMV) on or before the date of advertisement i.e. 26.09.2025.
