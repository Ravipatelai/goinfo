Bihar DElEd Entrance Exam Result 2025 Notice

All eligible candidates can download result online at BSEB DElEd official website i.e. https://biharboardonline.com or https://secondary.biharboardonline.com from 26th November 2025 onwards for two year DElEd entrance examination 2025. After uploading of result at board website you can download your result with Application Number and Password only.

Bihar DElEd Admission Process 2025

According to the merit list prepared on the basis of marks obtained in the DElEd Joint Entrance Examination conducted by the Bihar School Examination Board, for admission in Diploma in Elementary Education (D.El.Ed.) course in all the government and non-government affiliated teacher education institutions of Bihar, as per the reservation rules of the Government of Bihar, keeping in view the seats reserved for Urdu and Arts/ Commerce and Science subjects by the Government and the preference given by the candidates for the college, the colleges will be allotted to the candidates in online computerized manner.

Bihar DElEd Minimum Qualifying Marks 2025

The minimum qualifying marks for passing the D.El.Ed Joint Entrance Examination will be 35% for unreserved category candidates and 30% for reserved category candidates.

BSEB DElEd Result Normalization Process 2025

Competitive examinations conducted online, with a relatively large number of candidates, are held in multiple shifts/dates. Due to the varying difficulty levels of question papers in different shifts, a normalization method is adopted before preparing the results, so that candidates in any particular shift do not suffer any significant advantage or disadvantage. Therefore, the results will be published using the normalization method in this examination as well.

How To Download Bihar DElEd Result 2025

There are following simple steps for online downloading of Bihar DElEd Entrance Exam 2025 result as under described:

  1. Initially open BSEB DElEd official website https://secondary.biharboardonline.com into your computer or phone browser.
  2. In the top of home page under Important Links section click on the given link as "Click here for Result D.El.Ed. Joint Entrance Test, 2025"
  3. You will be redirected to result download page of Bihar DElEd Entrance Examination 2025.
  4. In the result download page under Login section enter your Application Number and Date of Birth (DDMMYYYY) correctly in the specified input field.
  5. Next fill Captcha Code as shown in that page correctly to proceed further.
  6. Finally click on Login button to download your result from BSEB DElEd server.
