BSPHCL Technician Grade 3 Result 2025 Notice

After the completion of the examination the result, score card and cut off marks of the examination will be made available on the company's website from 06th October 2025 onwards for Technician Grade 3 recruitment examination against ENN 05/2024. The result can be downloaded online by using Applicant's Login ID and Password only from the BSPHCL official website i.e https://www.bsphcl.co.in.

How To Download BSPHCL Technician Grade 3 Result 2025

There are following simple steps for downloading of online Result of BSPHCL Technician Grade 3 recruitment examination 2025 as under described:

  1. At first open Bihar State Power Holding Company Ltd (BSPHCL) official website https://bsedc.bihar.gov.in
  2. In the middle of Home Page under Important Links section click on the given link as "Download Result Against ENN-05/2024 (Post-Tech Gr.-III)".
  3. You will be redirected to Result download page of BSPHCL Technician Grade 3 Recruitment Examination 2025.
  4. In the Result login page enter your Registration No./ Login ID and Password correctly in the specified input field.
  5. Next enter Captcha Code as shown in the page correctly to proceed further.
  6. Finally click on Login button to download your Result from BSPHCL server.
