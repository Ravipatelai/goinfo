Eligibility Criteria For TSLPRB FSL Recruitment 2025

Scientific Officer (Physical / General)

M.Sc. with Physics or Forensic Science with Physics as special subject with minimum 65% aggregate marks. Post Graduation in Forensic Science should have studied Physics as one of the subjects at Graduation level.

Scientific Officer (Chemical)

M.Sc. with Chemistry or Forensic Science with Chemistry as special subject with minimum 65% aggregate marks. Post Graduation in Forensic Science should have studied Chemistry / Toxicology as one of the subjects at Graduation level.

Scientific Officer (Biology / Serology)

M.Sc. with Biology or Genetics or Zoology or Botany or Micro-Biology or Bio-Technology or Bio-Chemistry or Psychology (M.A / M.Sc.) or Forensic Science with specialization in Biology / Serology / DNA with minimum 65% aggregate marks.

Scientific Officer (Computers)

M.Sc. with Computer Science or M.Tech (EEE / ECE / Cyber Security / Cyber Forensics / CSC / IT) or MCA or Forensic Science (Computers) with minimum 65% aggregate marks. MCA / Forensic Science/ Physical Sciences/ Computers as a specialization at Graduation level.

Scientific Assistant (Physical / General)

M.Sc. with Physics or Forensic Science with Physics as specialized subject with minimum 60% aggregate marks. Post Graduation in Forensic Science should have studied Physics as one of the subjects at Graduation level.

Scientific Assistant (Chemical)

M.Sc. with Chemistry or Forensic Science with Chemistry as specialized subject with minimum 60% aggregate marks. Post Graduation in Forensic Science should have studied Chemistry/ Toxicology as one of the subjects at Graduation level.

Scientific Assistant (Biology / Serology)

M.Sc. with Biology/ Zoology/ Botany/ Micro-Biology/ Bio-Chemistry/ Bio-Technology/ Genetics/ Psychology (M.A) or Forensic Science with Biology/ Zoology/ Serology/ DNA/ Botany/ Micro-Biology/ Bio-Chemistry/ Bio-Technology/ Psychology as a specialized subject with minimum 60% aggregate marks.

Scientific Assistant (Computers)

M.Sc with Computer Science or M.Tech (EEE/ ECE/ Cyber Security/ Cyber Forensics/ CSC/ IT) or MCA or Forensic Science with Computers as a special subject with minimum 60% aggregate marks. Post Graduation in MCA/ Forensic Science/ Physical Sciences/ Computers as a specialization at Graduation level.

Laboratory Technician (Physical / General)

B.Sc. with Physics/ B.Sc. Forensic Science. Candidates who studied B.Sc. Forensic Science should have passed Intermediate with Physics as one of the subject.

Laboratory Technician (Chemical)

B.Sc. with Chemistry or B.Sc. Forensic Science. Candidates who studied B.Sc. Forensic Science should have passed intermediate with Chemistry as one of the subject.

Laboratory Technician (Biology / Serology)

B.Sc. with Biology or Zoology or Bio-Chemistry or Bio-Technology or Microbiology or Botany or Genetics or B.Sc. (Medical Lab Technician with Intermediate Bi PC group) / B.Sc. Forensic Science.
