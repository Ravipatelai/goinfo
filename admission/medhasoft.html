Medhasoft Bihar Scholarship Apply medhasoft.bih.nic.in

Updated On : 22 December 2024 04:45 PM

Medhasoft, Education Department of Bihar invites online applications form for Bihar Scholarship 2024 from Matric, Inter and Gradation Pass candidates for Protshan Yojana year 2024. All eligible male and female candidates can apply at Medha Soft official portal medhasoft.bih.nic.in for Mukhyamantri Balak/ Balika (10th Passed), Mukhyamantri Balika (Inter +2), Mukhyamantri Medhavriti Yojana and Mukhyamantri Kanya Uthan Yojana: Mukhyamantri Balika (Snatak) Protsahan Yojana 2024.

Interested candidates read the full notification carefully, before applying online.

Medhasoft Bihar Scholarship 2024 Eligibility Criteria

1. Mukhyamantri Balak/ Balika (10th Passed) Protsahan Yojana 2024

The candidates who have passed matriculation examination from Bihar School Examination Board in Year 2024 only.

2. Mukhyamantri Kanya Uthan Yojana: Mukhyamantri Balika (Intermediate +2) Protsahan Yojana 2024

The candidates who have passed intermediate examination from Bihar School Examination Board in Year 2024 only.

3. Mukhyamantri Kanya Uthan Yojana: Mukhyamantri Balika (Snatak) Protsahan Yojana 2023

4. Mukhyamantri Medhavriti Yojana 2024

The candidates who have passed intermediate examination from Bihar School Examination Board in Year 2024 only. (Only SC and ST girl students who are passed with 1st and 2nd class.)

How To Apply Online For Scholarship Through Medhasoft

  • Eligible students will be able to register through ekalyan portal https://medhasoft.bih.nic.in developed by NIC.
  • User ID and password will be available on the registered mobile number and email ID of the student after verification of the information entered at the time of registration.
  • After receiving the user ID and password, the student will be able to login to the portal again and fill the form.
  • Keep in mind that the bank account should be opened in the eligible student's own name and this account should be operated in any branch of Nationalized Bank, Recognized Private Bank or India Post Payment Bank located in Bihar.
  • Eligible students will be able to register themselves for the benefit of the above scheme before closing date.
