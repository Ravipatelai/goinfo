SSC JE Admit Card 2025 Exam City Download (Live)

Updated On : 26 November 2025 10:35 AM

Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has released online admit card and exam city slip for recruitment to the post of Junior Engineer JE (Civil, Mechanical & Electrical) for 1340 vacancies for Organizations/ Offices of the Government of India. The Commission has decided to conduct the CBT (Paper-I) examination from 03rd to 06th December 2025 across the country as per the notified examination center by the Commission. All eligible applicants can download their Exam City Slip online at SSC official website ssc.gov.in from 25th November 2025 onwards by using registration number and password for Junior Engineer (JE) Examination 2025.

SSC JE Exam City Slip 2025 Information

Information about the Junior Engineer Examination indicating the time table and City/ Centre of examination for the candidates will be uploaded on the website of the Commission about 10 days before the date of examination. If any candidate does not find his detail on the website of the Commission, one week before the date of examination, he must immediately contact the Regional Office concerned of the Commission with proof of having submitted his application. Failure to do so will deprive him of any claim for consideration.

SSC JE Exam Date 2025

The Computer Based Examination of Junior Engineer (Civil, Mechanical and Electrical) Examination, 2025 will be held between 03rd to 06th December 2025 across the country as per schedule notified by the commission.

SSC JE Admit Card 2025 Instructions

The Admit Card for Junior Engineer (Civil, Mechanical, Electrical) Exam 2025 (Paper-I) will tentatively be available for download before 03-04 days from the date of Examination. The same can be accessed through the designated login module on the website of the Commission (https://ssc.gov.in) in a similar manner.

In addition to the Admission Certificate, it is mandatory to carry at least two passport-size recent colour photographs and an Original valid Photo-ID proof having Date of Birth (DoB) as printed on the Admission Certificate.

How To Download SSC JE Admit Card 2025

There are following simple steps for online downloading of Junior Engineer (Civil, Mechanical, Electrical) Exam 2025 (Paper-I) admit card as under described:

  1. At first open Staff Selection Commission (SSC) official website i.e. https://ssc.gov.in
  2. In the middle of Home Page under Quick Links section click on Admit Card tab and select Download E-Admit Card of Junior Engineer (Civil, Mechanical, Electrical) Exam 2025 (Paper-I).
  3. You will be redirected to admit card download page of SSC JE Exam 2025.
  4. In the admit card download page under Login to your Account section enter your Registration Number and Password correctly in the specified input field.
  5. Next enter Captcha as shown in the page correctly to proceed further.
  6. Finally click on Login button to access your dashboard and click on download admit card button.
