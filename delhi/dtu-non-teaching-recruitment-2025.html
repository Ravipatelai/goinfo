DTU Non Teaching Recruitment 2025 Apply For JOA, OA

Post Date : 11 November 2025 04:45 PM

Delhi Technological University (DTU) invites online applications for non teaching recruitment to the post of Junior Office Assistant (JOA) and Office Assistant (OA) / Data Entry Operator (DEO) for 66 vacancies in group C category. All eligible and interested candidates can apply online at DTU official website at dtu.ac.in from 10th November 2025 to till the application last date 30th November 2025 for Non-Teaching Ministerial Group C Posts Recruitment 2025.

Interested candidates read the full notification carefully, before applying online.

DTU Non Teaching Post Recruitment Eligibility 2025

Junior Office Assistant (JOA) :

  1. Bachelor Degree from a recognized university.
  2. Typing speed of 35 w.p.m. in English or 30 w.p.m. in Hindi on computer.

Office Assistant (OA) / Data Entry Operator (DEO) :

  1. Bachelor Degree from a recognized university with at least two years of relevant experience in Level-2 (7th CPC) in a Central/ State Government/ University/ R&D Institution/ Autonomous Body/ Public Sector Undertaking.
  2. Typing speed of 35 w.p.m. in English or 30 w.p.m. in Hindi on computer.
