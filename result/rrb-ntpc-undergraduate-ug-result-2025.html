RRB NTPC Undergraduate Result 2025 Notice

Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) has declared 1st Stage Computer Based test (CBT-I) result, cut off marks and score card online for the various Posts of Non Technical Popular Categories (Under Graduate) on 21st November 2025. Candidates can download the result online at any RRB Recruitment zonal official website using the registration number and password. The 1st Stage Computer Based Test (CBT) was conducted during the period from 07th August 2025 to 9th September 2025 by Railway Recruitment Boards for the posts of NTPC against CEN No. 06/2024.

How To Download RRB NTPC UG Result 2025

There are following simple steps for online downloading of RRB NTPC Undergraduate Recruitment Examination 2025 result as under described:

  1. At first open Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) any regional official website (s).
  2. In the middle of Home Page under Important Updates section click on the given link as "CEN 06/2024 (NTPC) Result Link: The link will be active from 21st November 2025".
  3. You will redirected to result download page of RRB NTPC Undergraduate Recruitment Examination 2025.
  4. In the result download page under Login section enter your Registration Number and Date of Birth correctly in the specified input field.
  5. Next enter Captcha Code as shown in the page correctly to proceed further.
  6. Finally click on Submit button to download your result from RRB Recruitment server and now you can save a copy in PDF or print out it for future reference.
