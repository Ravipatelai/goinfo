RRB Ministerial & Isolated Categories Result 2025

Updated On : 25 November 2025 10:00 PM

Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) invites online applications for recruitment of the various posts of ministerial and isolated categories for 1036 vacancies in pay level-2 to 8 for Centralised Employment Notice (CEN) No 07/2024. All eligible and interested Indian citizens can apply online at Railway RRB recruitment official website rrbapply.gov.in from 07th January 2025 to till the application last date 28th February 2025 for ministerial and isolated categories recruitment in all Railway Recruitment Boards.

Interested candidates read the full notification carefully, before applying online.

RRB Ministerial and Isolated Categories Criteria 2025

Candidates should have requisite minimum Educational/ Technical/ Professional qualifications indicated for the posts in Annexure-A of this CEN, from a recognized Board/University/institute/ Authority as on the closing date for online submission of applications i.e. 06/02/2025. Those awaiting results of the final examination of the prescribed minimum educational qualification whose final results will be declared after the closing date of online submission of application SHOULD NOT apply.
