ECGC PO Recruitment Eligibility Criteria 2025

Probationary Officers (Generalists) :

Bachelor's Degree in any discipline or any equivalent qualification.

Probationary Officers (Specialists) Rajbhasha/ Hindi :

  1. Master's degree in Hindi/ Hindi Translation, with English as core/ Elective/ Major subject at the Bachelor's Degree level with minimum 55% for SC/ ST and 60% for others; OR
  2. Master's degree in English with Hindi as core/ elective/ major subject at the bachelor's degree. OR
  3. Master's degree in any discipline with English and Hindi as core/ elective/ major subjects at the bachelor's degree level with minimum 55%for SC/ ST and 60% for others. OR
  4. Master's degree in both English and Hindi/ Hindi translation with minimum 55%for SC/ ST and 60% for others.
