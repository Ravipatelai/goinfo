AIIMS CRE 4 Recruitment 2025 Notification, Apply Online

Post Date : 16 November 2025 10:00 AM

All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), New Delhi invites online applications for Common Recruitment Examination- 4 (CRE-4) 2025 for Recruitment to the various Group B and C posts at Participating AIIMS and Central Government Hospitals under Ministry of Health and Family Welfare. All eligible and interested male and female candidates can apply online at AIIMS recruitment official website aiimsexams.ac.in from 14th November 2025 to till the application last date 02nd December 2025 for AIIMS CRE 4 Recruitment 2025.

Interested candidates read the full notification carefully, before applying online.
