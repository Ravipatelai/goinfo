CCRH Recruitment 2025 Apply Online For Group A, B & C

Post Date : 16 November 2025 09:00 PM

Central Council for Research in Homoeopathy (CCRH) invites online applications for recruitment to the post of Research Officer, Junior Librarian, Pharmacist, X-Ray Technician, LDC, Driver, Assistant Research Officer, Staff Nurse, MLT, JMLT and Junior Stenographer for 90 vacancies in group A, B and C category under 27 Institutes/ Units across the country. All eligible and interested candidates can apply online at CCRH official website at ccrhindia.ayush.gov.in or ccrhonline.in or eapplynow.com from 05th November 2025 to till the application last date 26th November 2025 for Group A, B and C posts recruitment examination 2025.

Interested candidates read the full notification carefully, before applying online.

CCRH Group A, B and C Recruitment Eligibility 2025

Research Officer (Homoeopathy)

  1. MD in Homoeopathy.
  2. Enrolment on the Central Register of CCH or State Register of Homoeopathy.

Maximum Age: 40 Years

Research Officer (Endocrinology)

  1. Master's degree in science in Zoology/ M.Pharma (Pharmacology).
  2. 03 years research/ teaching experience in the field of Bio- Medical Sciences form a recognized Institute/ Laboratory/ University aft er obtaining Master Degree in the prescribed d discipline.

Maximum Age: 40 Years

Research Officer (Pathology)

M.D. (Pathology) from MCI recognized institution.

Maximum Age: 40 Years

Junior Librarian

  1. Graduation in Library Science from recognized University.
  2. One year experience in a Library of recognized institution/ University/ Department.

Maximum Age: 25 Years

Pharmacist

  1. 12th Standard pass or equivalent with physics Chemistry or Biology.
  2. Diploma or Certificate course i Homoeopathy Pharmacy of least one year duration from recognized institution.

Age Limit: 18-25 Years

X-Ray Technician

  1. Certificate in X-Ray Technology of minimum two years' duration from a recognized Institution.
  2. One year experience in handling X-Ray plant in a recognized Hospital or Institution.

Maximum Age: 25 Years

Lower Division Clerk (LDC)

Educational Qualification: 12th Class pass or equivalent.

Skill test Norms on Computer: A typing speed of 35 words per minute in English or 30 words per minute in Hindi on computer (Time allowed: 10 minutes).

(35 w.p.m. and 30 w.p.m. correspond to 10500 KDPH / 9000 KDPH on average of 5 key depressions for each word.)

Age Limit: 18-27 Years

Driver

  1. Pass Middle School Examination from a recognized school.
  2. Valid Driving License for Light and Heavy Vehicles.
  3. Experience for about two years in the line.

Maximum Age: 25 Years

Assistant Research Officer (Pharmacognosy)

Master's degree in science in Botany/ Medicinal plants or M.Pharma (pharmacognosy).

Maximum Age: 30 Years

Staff Nurse

    1. B.Sc. (Hons.) Nursing/ B.Sc Nursing OR B. Sc. (Post certificate)/ Post Basic B.Sc. Nursing.
    2. Registered as a Nurse or Nurse and Mid-wife (RN or RN&RM) with State/ Indian Nursing Council.
    3. Six months' experience in minimum fifty bedded hospital after acquiring the educational qualification mentioned above. OR
    1. Diploma in General Nursing & Midwifery.
    2. Registered as a Nurse or Nurse and Mid-wife (RN or RN&RM) with State/ Indian Nursing Council.
    3. Two years' experience in minimum fifty bedded hospital after acquiring the educational qualification mentioned above.

Maximum Age: 30 Years

Medical Laboratory Technologist (MLT)

Bachelor's degree in medical laboratory science from recognized University/ Institution with 2-year relevant experience.

Maximum Age: 35 Years

Junior Medical Laboratory Technologist (JMLT)

10 + 2 with Science Subject and DMLT from any Government Recognized Institution with 1 year relevant experience.

Maximum Age: 28 Years

Junior Stenographer

Educational Qualification:

  1. 12th class pass or equivalent from recognized Board or University.
  2. Skill Test Norms on Computer

Dictation: 10 minutes @ 80 words per minute

Transcription: 50 minutes (English) and 65 minutes (Hindi)

Age Limit: 18-27 Years
