UPSC EPFO EO / AO, APFO Eligibility Criteria 2025

Enforcement Officer (EO) / Accounts Officer (AO)

Bachelor’s Degree in any subject from recognised university or institute.

Maximum Age : 30 Years

Assistant Provident Fund Commissioner (APFC)

Degree of a recognised University or Equivalent.

Maximum Age : 35 Years
