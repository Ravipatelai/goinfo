JSSC Scientific Assistant DV List 2025 JSACE

Updated On : 25 November 2025 09:15 PM

Jharkhand Staff Selection Commission (JSSC) has released online answer key for recruitment to the post of Scientific Assistant for 23 vacancies through Jharkhand Scientific Assistant Competitive Examination (JSACE) 2025 under Department of Home, Prison and Disaster Management. All eligible and interested male and female candidates can download answer key online at JSSC official website jssc.jharkhand.gov.in from 27th September 2025 onwards for Scientific Assistant recruitment in JSSC.

Interested candidates read the full notification carefully, before applying online.

JSSC Scientific Assistant Eligibility Criteria 2025

Minimum Educational Qualification :-

  • M.Sc. in related subject with 55% marks from a recognized University

    OR

  • M.Sc. in forensic science with 55% marks from a recognized University and standing in B.Sc. (related subject) at Graduate level.

Essential Qualification :-

  • It is mandatory to have a certificate course in computer (minimum 6 months Diploma in Computer Application) from any institute registered by the State Government / Government of India.
  • Apart from this, it is mandatory to obtain 30% marks by appearing in Paper-1 (Computer Knowledge and Operation) under the main examination.

See notification for more qualification details.
