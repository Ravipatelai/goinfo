Updated On : 26 November 2025 06:00 PM

Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) invites online applications for recruitment of stations master, goods train manager, junior account assistant-cum-typist, TC, trains clerk, junior clerk-cum-typist and others under Non Technical Popular Categories (NTPC) graduate and undergraduate posts for CEN No. 05/2024 and 06/2024. All eligible and interested Indian nationals can apply online at Railway RRB recruitment official website rrbapply.gov.in from 14th September 2024 to till the application last date 27th October 2024 for NTPC Posts recruitment in all Railway Recruitment Boards.

Interested candidates read the full notification carefully, before applying online.

Railway NTPC Eligibility Criteria 2024

For CEN 05/2024 (Graduate Posts) :

Chief Commercial Cum Ticket Supervisor / Station Master/ Goods Train manager : Degree from recognized University or its equivalent.

Junior Accounts Assistant Cum Typist / Senior Clerk Cum Typist : Degree from recognised University or its equivalent. Typing proficiency in English / Hindi on Computer is essential.

For CEN 06/2024 (Under Graduate Posts) :

Commercial Cum Ticket Clerk : 12th or its equivalent with not less than 50% marks in the aggregate. 50% marks is not to be insisted upon in case of SC/ ST/ Persons with Benchmark Disability/Ex-servicemen and candidates Who possess qualifications higher than 12th.

Accounts Clerk Cum Typist : 12th or its equivalent with not less than 50%marks in the aggregate. 50% marks is not to be insisted upon in case of SC/ ST/ Persons with Benchmark Disability/Ex-servicemen and candidates who possess qualifications higher than 12th. Typing proficiency in English / Hindi on Computer is essential.

Junior Clerk Cum Typist : 12th or its equivalent with not less than 50% marks in the aggregate. 50% marks is not to be insisted upon in case of SC/ ST/ Persons with Benchmark Disability/ Ex-servicemen and candidates who possess qualifications higher than 12th. Typing proficiency in English / Hindi on Computer is essential.

Trains Clerk (TC) : 12th or its equivalent with not less than 50% marks in the aggregate. 50% marks is not to be insisted upon in case of SC/ ST/ Persons with Benchmark Disability/Ex-servicemen and candidates Who possess qualifications higher than 12th.
